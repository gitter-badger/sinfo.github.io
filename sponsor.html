Description

In case you are not familiar with us, we invite you to visit our homepage presenting the several elements of our event. You can also visit the XXI SINFO website, the 2014 edition.

Reasons to sponsor

  • To give information about the company to potential future employees, graduated in a college with excellent national and international renown.
  • To project the company's image and brand, in the media (in previous editions, we were advertised, or covered, by Expresso, Correio da Manhã, RTP2, SIC Radical and many others).
  • To publicize opportunities for internships and MsC/Phd theses offered by your company
  • Highlight the company's brand, compared to its competitors.

Keynote Sponsorship

The sponsorship and the company’s name and logo are mentioned and shown both in the begining and at the end of the talk, during the prizes draw. Presentation		-√√√

Presentation

You will be able to make a company’s presentation to an audience of students that will be at the event. The presentation allows the company to introduce and explain to the students their field of work, the projects they are or were working on, but also the job opportunities that might be available to the students in the company.

  • 45min session divided as such:
    1. 5 minutos para a empresa se dar a conhecer.
    2. 5min to present the company and get the students to learn a little more about the company itself;
    3. 30min presentation (Preferably by Engineers or LEIC/IST Alumni);
    4. Semi-technical presentations tend to be preferred by the students;
    5. 10min for Q&A.
  • Congress Center – rooms with capacity for 50 or 70 students.
